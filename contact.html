Frequently Asked Questions

How do you choose which AI tools to review?

I focus on tools that are either free/freemium, have significant user bases, or represent important developments in AI. I prioritize tools that students and beginners can actually access and benefit from.

Do you accept paid promotions?

Yes, but only for tools and services that I genuinely believe provide value to my audience. I maintain complete editorial control and always disclose sponsorships transparently.

How can I suggest an AI tool for review?

Feel free to send me suggestions through the contact form above or via DM on Instagram. I love discovering new tools from my community!

Can I repost your content?

Please reach out first! I'm happy to collaborate on cross-promotion and often create custom content for partnerships.

Do you offer 1-on-1 AI tutoring?

Currently, I focus on creating educational content for broader audiences, but I'm always open to discussing group workshops or custom educational projects.

How often do you post new content?

I aim for 3-4 posts per week across platforms, plus weekly detailed experiment write-ups on the Lab Notes section.
